# The Freemium Community Playbook

A data-verified playbook for building a freemium Skool community, built in public with pilots.

## Builds

- **Build 1:** The full launch arc, from theory through your first 100 members. 4 modules, 12 exercises.
- **Build 2:** Scaling and sustaining: onboarding, live sessions, churn prevention, revenue stacking, and proof-based communication. 5 modules, 22 exercises.

## What This Is

Every claim in this playbook went through a three-step process. First, we researched it. Second, we verified it by tracing numbers back to original sources. Third, we tried to disprove it. What survived that process is in the playbook.

Each build ships when it's ready. Co-pilots follow along, run the exercises on their own communities, and report back what works and what doesn't. Your results become part of the playbook.

## Who This Is For

Creators, solopreneurs, and practitioners who have expertise people ask them about but no scalable way to monetize it.
