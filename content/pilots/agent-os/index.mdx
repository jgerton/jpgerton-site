# Agent OS

Agent OS is a toolkit for knowledge workers who use AI daily. The first tool is **vault-intake**: a Claude-powered skill that captures YouTube videos, articles, transcripts, and other content directly into your Obsidian vault as structured, linked notes.

More tools and verticals follow. Everything you capture stays in your vault, in plain markdown, under your control.

## Where this fits in YCAH

You Craft and AI Helps operates on three tiers:

- **Basic** -- self-serve AI tools you can use right now, including vault-intake. No subscription required beyond YCAH membership.
- **Premium** -- guided workflows, live sessions, and community accountability for practitioners building with AI.
- **Pro** -- personalized builds and direct collaboration.

vault-intake is a Basic-tier tool. This pilot section is where you go deeper: install it, configure it for your vault, run your first real capture, and report what you find.

## The vault portability principle

Your knowledge lives in your vault. vault-intake writes plain markdown files with YAML frontmatter and wikilinks. No vendor lock-in, no proprietary format, no sync service required. If you stop using vault-intake tomorrow, every note it wrote is still readable, searchable, and linkable in Obsidian.

This is intentional. AI tools that own your knowledge create dependency. Tools that write to your vault extend it.

- Configure it for your vault domains and content types
- Run real captures, not demos
- Report what worked, what felt off, and what you wish it did differently

Your responses go directly to Jon. Patterns across pilots drive the next build scope.
